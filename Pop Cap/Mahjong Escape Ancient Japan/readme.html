Minimum System Requirements

-------------------

* Windows XP/VISTA/ME/98SE/2000

* 350Mhz Pentium or greater CPU

* 128 MB RAM or greater

* 16-bit Graphics and Sound card

* DirectX7 or later

* 25 MB free hard drive space

Game Summary

---------------

Escape to the land of the rising sun! Relax & play the second game in the hit series Mahjong Escape™. Your tile-matching adventure begins over 10,000 years ago in Ancient Japan. Match the Magic Gold tiles and travel through 12 Japanese era's, unlocking ancient wisdom on your quest to collect the lost Emperors’ treasures.

Includes 2 ways to play, Emperor’s Adventure & Classic Mah Jong solitaire.  Enjoy over 200 unique levels, 6 tileset designs, 21 hand-painted backgrounds to unlock, 3 difficulty settings (relaxed, normal, and expert) and a relaxing soundtrack. Escape today!

Troubleshooting

---------------

This software has been tested to operate on the above system requirements. If you experience any problems such as system crashes, any graphics issues or error messages, please try one of the following:

1) Update your computer's video or sound card drivers. This can be done by identifying the type of sound card and video card your computer is using and finding the manufacturer's web site where new drivers may be made available for download.

2) Upgrade DirectX. This is a Microsoft component and can improve the graphics, security, and performance of your Windows operating system.

3) If 1 and 2 do not fix graphic problems please go to "Options" menu available on the main menu and UNCHECK "Use 3D Hardware Acceleration".

4) If you are still experiencing graphical display problems (such as mixed up images or your mouse cursor flickering), you can try forcing the game to start without 3-D hardware acceleration by holding down the "Shift" key when you start the game.
